THE PERSON BEHIND THE PIXELS

I bridge the gap between people and the products they use.

I am a UX/UI Designer driven by a deep curiosity about human behavior — how people think, what they expect, and where they get lost. My approach is rooted in critical observation and a refusal to accept "good enough" as a standard.

I view design not just as an aesthetic layer, but as a problem-solving framework. I don't just ask what users need — I ask why, and I let that question shape every decision. A working knowledge of frontend development informs how I design — I understand the constraints, speak the language, and create solutions that are as realistic as they are thoughtful.

Core Philosophy

I believe that the most powerful designs are often the ones you don't notice—the ones that work exactly how you expect them to, without explanation.
